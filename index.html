About
As someone who has always loved logic and reason, I have found my perfect career in software engineering. Each problem I come across is another little puzzle to solve. The beauty of coding is that computers are entirely rational. If there is ever an issue, it is not because my tools are broken, rather my understanding of the logic is flawed. And I can't stop until I fix that gap.
I am currently a full stack developer, specializing in JavaScript and the NERDS (Node.js, Express, React, Databases using SQL) stack, and I am always open to exploring new technologies.
On a personal level, I am a huge basketball fan (both playing and watching). I also enjoy skiing and hanging out with my girlfriend and dog!
